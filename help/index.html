1. How to play?

Setup

The game is played on a 6x7 board, with 48 pieces divided into two different colors (red-black, red-yellow, and red-blue are popular).

Gameplay

Each player takes turns filling up the board with their pieces falling vertically. You can only place a piece in a column that isn't filled.

Winning

The game is won by the player that gets 4 pieces (discs/stones) in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Draw

If the board is filled and there is no winner, the game will be a draw.

Time

Original 4 In A Row doesn't have any time limits, but on 4InARow.ai, we have added time as a feature. There's starting time and bonus time. Starting time is the time the clock starts with, and bonus time is the time a player gets after finishing a move. Time is by default set to 1m, with 5s bonus.

Rating

The website uses a ranking system known as Glicko-2 to calculate the rating points for each player. This rating is used to determine the right player to play against in matches. You will play against someone with a similar rating as yourself, same as in chess
